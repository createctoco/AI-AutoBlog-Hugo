Begin by estimating the total number of units needed. For a typical parish, a bulk order might range from 200 to 5,000 rosaries, depending on the event. Before finalizing a church supply religious items bulk order, request physical samples. This allows you to assess bead finish, crucifix weight, string strength, and overall craftsmanship. Reputable factories will provide samples at a nominal cost, often refundable with the first bulk order.
