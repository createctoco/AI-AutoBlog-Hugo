### The St. Benedict Cross: More Than a Pendant

Here’s something most people don’t realize. The St. Benedict crucifix isn’t just a cross — it’s a sacramental. The medal on it has specific symbols. The raven, the cup with the serpent. Each element tells a story from St. Benedict’s life.

We make these crucifixes in brass, zinc alloy, and sterling silver. The brass ones? They develop a nice patina over time. Some customers love that. Others want shiny forever. We offer both finishes.

One thing I insist on: the engraving must be deep. Not surface-level. If someone runs their finger over the letters, they should feel the indent. That’s how you know it’ll last.
