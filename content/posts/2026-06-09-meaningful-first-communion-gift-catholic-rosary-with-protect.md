### Knots Matter More Than You Think

You might not care about knots. But your customers do.

We can do two types of knots between beads: the Peruvian knot (tight, clean, traditional) or the flat knot (a bit more modern, lies flatter against the hand). Most of our First Communion orders go with Peruvian knots — they’re what people expect from a classic rosary. But we’ve had some European buyers request flat knots, especially for younger kids who tend to fidget.

No extra charge for either. Just tell us what you want.
