## The problem with most pocket crucifixes on the market

They’re too thin. Or the corpus breaks off. Or the finish flakes. I’ve seen it all. One importer from the Philippines told me he stopped ordering from his previous supplier because the crucifixes bent when people sat down with them in their back pockets. That’s embarrassing for everyone.

We solved that by making our crucifixes a bit thicker. About 3mm at the thickest part of the cross. That may not sound like much, but it makes a difference. We also weld the corpus to the cross. Not glue. Not a cheap press fit. Actual welding. Then we hand-file the joint smooth. It takes longer, but you don’t get returns.
