## What to watch out for when sourcing

A few things we’ve learned over the years.

First, check the weight. Cheap pendants feel light and hollow. Ours have heft. Not heavy, but solid. You can feel the difference.

Second, check the clasp. We use lobster clasps with a small ring. Easy to open, but holds well. Some suppliers use thin wire clasps that break after a week. Not us.

Third, check the finish. Two-tone should be distinct — gold on one part, silver on the other. Not blended or smudged. We inspect each piece.

Fourth, check the pendant size. St. Michael pendants usually range from 20mm to 30mm. Too small and you can’t see the details. Too big and it looks like a medallion. We stick with 25mm — just right.
