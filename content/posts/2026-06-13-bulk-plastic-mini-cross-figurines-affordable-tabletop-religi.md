## Bulk Buying Tips From a Factory That's Done This a While

We've shipped to over 30 countries. Catholic buyers, Protestant churches, even some Orthodox communities. Here's what we've learned:

- **Start small, test quality.** Order 12-50 pieces first. See how they hold up.
- **Think about packaging.** A cross in a plain poly bag feels cheap. One in a branded box feels like a gift.
- **Consider the occasion.** Baptism crosses should be smaller. Funeral memorial crosses can be larger, maybe with a stand.
- **Ask about knot options.** For rosaries, we do Peruvian knot or flat knot. For cross pendants, the chain or cord matters.
