## What goes into a Catholic baby shower favor set

Most of the time, people come to us wanting a small rosary. Not the full 20-decade set, not something too bulky. Something that fits in a palm, slips into a pocket, or hangs off a diaper bag. The 8mm bead size is our go-to for this. It’s not too big, not too small. Feels substantial in the hand but doesn’t scream “I’m wearing a necklace.”

We use real solid wood for these. Not plastic, not resin pretending to be wood. Natural solid wood. Each bead gets hand-carved and hand-polished. You can feel the difference when you hold one. The grain is real. The weight is right. It’s not something that looks cheap after a few weeks.

## The knot matters. Yes, really.

You’d be surprised how many people ask about the knot. Some want the Peruvian knot — that’s the classic, tight, uniform look. Others prefer a flat knot — softer, more flexible, easier to wrap around a wrist. Both work. We can do either. Just tell us what you need.

If you’re ordering for a baby shower where the favors will be handed out by volunteers, the flat knot might be easier to handle. Less fuss. But if you want that traditional rosary feel, the Peruvian knot is the way to go. We’ve done both for the same client before. No problem.
